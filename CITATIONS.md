# Research & Documentation

This framework is built upon the findings of several critical security studies:

*   **Supply Chain Impact:** Analysis shows that campaigns targeting NPM and PyPI can affect over 2.6 billion weekly downloads.
*   **Worm Behavior:** The self-propagating "Shai-Hulud" worm identified in late 2025 serves as the primary threat model for this tracking framework.
*   **Detection Accuracy:** Modern knowledge-driven frameworks have achieved up to 99.50% accuracy in identifying malicious PyPI packages.
*   **Behavioral Clustering:** Research into clustering installation scripts has successfully identified hundreds of unknown malicious packages.
*   **Metadata Anomalies:** The use of metadata-based detection helps identify "TeamPCP" patterns before the payload is even executed.
*   **Structural Defenses:** Establishing reproducible builds remains the long-term goal for securing the ecosystem.
